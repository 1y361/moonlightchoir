About Moonlight Choir

Our story, mission, and ministry

Our Journey in Music

Founded in 2008, Moonlight Choir began with a simple vision: to share the love of God through harmonious voices. What started as a small group of friends singing at Sabbath services has grown into a vibrant choir ministry serving the SDA community.

Over the years, we've been blessed to minister through music at countless church services, concerts, community events, and special programs. Our repertoire spans traditional hymns, contemporary gospel, and original compositions.

Today, Moonlight Choir consists of over 20 dedicated members who share a passion for worship and musical excellence. We continue to grow, learn, and spread joy through our music.

Our Mission

Worship

To lead the congregation in heartfelt worship through music that honors God.

Ministry

To use our musical gifts to serve the church and community.

Growth

To develop musical skills and spiritual depth in our members.

Outreach

To share God's love through music in our community and beyond.
